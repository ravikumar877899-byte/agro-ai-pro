Irrigation

With clear skies and moderate humidity, maintain standard watering schedules. Deep watering at the roots in the early morning is highly recommended.

Disease Risk

Low risk for late blight due to lack of rain. However, watch out for powdery mildew in dense canopies if nighttime temperatures drop.

Pest Watch

Warm, sunny days may increase aphid and spider mite activity. Inspect the undersides of leaves frequently and apply organic soap if detected.
